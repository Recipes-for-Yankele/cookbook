# Miso Soup Freezer Balls

> Batch-prep miso soup in minutes, whenever you want it.

*Reference: https://www.okonomikitchen.com/miso-balls/*

---

## Ingredients

- 1 cup organic white miso
- 1½ tsp hondashi granules
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1 tsp tahini (sesame paste)
- Spring onions, chopped *(however much you have)*

---

## Method

1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl until fully combined. The hondashi granules will dissolve — that's fine.
2. Roll into balls slightly smaller than a golf ball. The soup is quite salty, so keep them small. → [[Techniques/Batch Freezer Prep|Batch Freezer Prep]]
3. Store in the freezer.

---

## To Use

- **Miso soup:** Drop 1 ball into hot water and stir until dissolved.
- **Spicy ramen broth:** 1 miso ball + 1 tsp hot pot base. Instant spicy soup, great for noodles.
