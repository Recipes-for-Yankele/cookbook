# Cheatcode Korean Chicken

> When you want Korean flavors without any real effort.

---

## Ingredients

- 2 portions frozen chicken nuggets
- 1 cup quick-cooking frozen veggie (broccoli, peas, or edamame)
- 2 tbsp gochujang
- 1 heaping tbsp garlic paste
- 1 heaping tbsp ginger paste
- 1 tsp chili oil
- Glug of sesame oil
- 1 tsp miso paste *(don't overdo it — it gets salty fast)*

---

## Method

1. Bake (or air-fry) chicken nuggets until done.
2. While nuggets are baking, heat a pot over medium. Add chili oil, then fry the ginger, garlic, gochujang, and miso together. → [[Techniques/Garlic and Ginger Paste Base|Garlic & Ginger Paste Base]] · [[Techniques/Blooming Spices|Blooming Spices]]
3. Add a splash of water to thin the sauce out.
4. About 4 minutes before the nuggets are done, add frozen veggies to the sauce. Cook through. Finish with sesame oil.
5. Once nuggets are done, toss them in the sauce.
6. Serve over white rice.
