# Baked Tofu & Crispy Rice Salad

> Extremely filling salad with two protein sources: edamame and tofu. Make extra peanut dressing — it's that good. Feels healthy as hell.

*Inspired by: https://pin.it/KAnrPqVDw and https://pin.it/5KWyB0j6c*

---

## Ingredients

### Salad Base
- 1–2 romaine hearts, chopped
- Frozen edamame, thawed
- 1 bell pepper or cucumber, chopped *(for crunch — cucumber preferred)*
- Spring onions, chopped
- Avocado *(optional — budget depending)*
- Sesame seeds *(for garnish)*

### Crispy Rice
- 1–2 cups cooked white rice *(leftover fridge rice works best)*
- Soy sauce
- Mirin
- Chili oil
- Garlic paste *(optional)*

### Baked Tofu
- 1 block firm or extra-firm tofu
- Soy sauce
- Mirin
- Chili oil

### Peanut Dressing
- Chunky peanut butter *(primary flavor — use a lot)*
- Fresh ginger paste *(primary flavor — use a lot)*
- Gochujang
- Toasted sesame oil
- Garlic paste
- Lime juice *(lemon works but lime is better)*
- Soy sauce
- Sesame paste (tahini)
- Mirin
- Tiny splash of rice vinegar
- Miso *(optional, but it would be good)*
- Water to thin

---

## Method

1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
2. **Prep tofu:** Grate on a box grater, or crumble with your hands and squeeze out as much water as possible. Grated gives the best texture. → [[Techniques/Pressing and Grating Tofu|Pressing & Grating Tofu]]
3. On a lined baking sheet, toss grated tofu with soy sauce, mirin, and chili oil.
4. On a separate lined baking sheet, toss cold rice with soy sauce, mirin, and chili oil. Add garlic paste if desired.
5. Bake both trays at 400°F, tossing periodically, until crispy — about 20–25 minutes. Rice will finish first. Use the broiler for extra crispiness.
6. While everything bakes, chop romaine, spring onions, and cucumber or bell pepper into a serving bowl.
7. Microwave frozen edamame in a bowl with a splash of water in 20-second intervals until thawed and warm.
8. Make peanut dressing: Combine peanut butter and ginger in a large proportion, then add the remaining ingredients to taste. Thin with water until pourable — it will emulsify on its own.
9. Let rice cool slightly before serving. Tofu can be served hot.
10. Dress the salad, then top with crispy rice and tofu like croutons for maximum crunch. Garnish with sesame seeds.

---

## Notes

- The tofu shrinks down significantly — you'll finish half a block and barely notice.
- Cucumber > bell pepper for crunch if you have it.
- Make extra peanut dressing. Seriously.
- Great pre-workout meal — the peanut butter makes it very filling.
- Open a window — the smoke alarm may go off.
