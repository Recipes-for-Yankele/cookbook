# Mayak Eggs

> Eggs were on sale. Now what?

---

## Ingredients

- 6–12 eggs *(scale based on how many servings you want)*
- 1 tsp white vinegar *(for boiling)*
- 2–4 tbsp white or red onion, finely chopped *(less than half an onion)*
- Garlic paste
- Soy sause
- Mirin
- Sesame paste (tahini)
- Chili oil
- Dried red chilies
- Water *(for the marinade)*

---

## Method

1. Soft-boil eggs: bring water to a boil, add vinegar, gently lower eggs in, and cook for **6 minutes**. → [[Techniques/Soft-Boiling Eggs|Soft-Boiling Eggs]]
2. Transfer immediately to an ice bath and peel once cool.
3. Combine all marinade ingredients — onion, garlic paste, tahini, chili oil, dried chilies, and enough water to submerge the eggs.
4. Place peeled eggs in the marinade. Refrigerate for at least a few hours, ideally overnight.
5. Serve over rice or as a snack.

---

## Notes

- The longer they marinate, the more flavor they absorb.
- 2–3 eggs per portion is a good guideline.
